Clarity
What we do — and what we don't
Transparency is important to us, our users, and our partners.

What We Do

  • Build software for personal budgeting and expense tracking
  • Provide financial literacy content and educational resources
  • Enable secure, read-only bank account aggregation
  • Help users set and monitor savings goals
  • Facilitate group savings tracking (stokvels)
  • Generate spending reports and insights
  • Charge subscription fees for premium software features

What We Don't Do

  • Provide financial advice, investment advice, or tax advice
  • Sell financial products (insurance, investments, loans)
  • Hold, manage, or transmit client funds
  • Act as an intermediary for financial transactions
  • Operate as a bank, insurer, or Financial Services Provider
  • Guarantee investment returns or savings outcomes
  • Share or sell personal data to third parties for marketing
Regulatory
Our Regulatory Status

🏛️ Regulatory Status

MyRandWise operates as a technology and software company. We are not registered as a Financial Services Provider (FSP) with the South African Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA), nor are we required to be, as we do not provide financial advice, sell financial products, or hold client funds.

Our activities fall under general software development and educational publishing. We are in the process of formalising our company registration with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) of South Africa.
