Risk
Risk Profile for Insurers & Brokers
Key information for professional indemnity, cyber liability, and D&O insurance assessment.

🟢 Regulatory Risk — LOW

We do not require an FSCA FSP license because we do not provide financial advice, sell financial products, or hold client funds. Our activities are software development and education — regulated under general company and consumer protection law, not financial services law.

🟡 Cyber Liability — MEDIUM

We aggregate read-only bank data via Yodlee (a Nasdaq-listed, SOC 2 compliant provider). We do not store banking credentials. We hold transactional data and personal information under POPIA. Cyber insurance is recommended and being sourced.

🟢 Financial Risk — LOW

We do not hold client funds. All transactions are subscription payments processed via secure payment gateways (PayFast / Stripe). No credit risk, no investment risk, no balance sheet exposure to client assets.

🟢 Professional Indemnity — LOW

We explicitly disclaim the provision of financial advice in our Terms of Service. All educational content is general in nature and includes appropriate warnings. Users make their own financial decisions.
